Our Programs

Age-appropriate programs designed for optimal development

Toddler Care (18 months - 2 years)

A gentle introduction to structured learning with focus on sensory exploration and social skills development.

  • Sensory play activities
  • Basic motor skill development
  • Social interaction
  • Story time and music

Preschool (3 - 4 years)

Comprehensive early learning program preparing children for kindergarten through play-based activities.

  • Pre-literacy and numeracy
  • Creative arts and crafts
  • Science exploration
  • Physical development

Pre-K (4 - 5 years)

Advanced preparation for kindergarten with structured learning and independent skill development.

  • Reading and writing readiness
  • Mathematical concepts
  • Critical thinking skills
  • School readiness
